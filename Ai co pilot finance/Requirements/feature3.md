# Feature 3: Automated Expense & Payables Management  

This feature provides micro-business owners with full control over outgoing payments and expense tracking. It ensures that every outflow is accounted for, categorized, and optimized for cashflow stability.

The objective is not just recording expenses.  
The objective is proactive expense visibility, control, and predictability.

This feature transforms expenses from opaque outflows into structured financial intelligence.

---

## 3.1 Epic – Expense Lifecycle Control  

This epic manages the full lifecycle of each expense, from recognition to settlement.

---

### 3.1.1 Story – Streamlined Expense Recording  

The system shall allow users to record expenses effortlessly, supporting:

- Direct bank import recognition  
- Manual entry with minimal fields  
- Expense categorization by type or function  
- Supplier or payee selection  
- Tax and compliance tagging (optional)  

**Functional Requirements:**

- Automatic vendor suggestion based on transaction history  
- AI-assisted categorization  
- Duplicate detection  
- Timestamp and metadata storage  

The process must be quick, intuitive, and free from accounting jargon.

---

### 3.1.2 Story – Payables Tracking & Status  

Every expense shall have a status that tracks its progress:

- Pending  
- Scheduled  
- Paid  
- Overdue  

The system shall:

- Highlight overdue payments  
- Display payment due dates in dashboards  
- Track partial payments  
- Allow quick adjustment of scheduled payments  

The objective is total visibility of payables without complex ledger systems.

---

### 3.1.3 Story – Recurring Expense Automation  

The platform shall detect and manage recurring expenses, including:

- Rent or lease payments  
- Subscriptions  
- Salaries or contractor fees  
- Utilities  

Capabilities include:

- Automatic scheduling  
- Notification prior to execution  
- Integration into cashflow projections  

This ensures operational predictability and reduces late payments.

---

## 3.2 Epic – Supplier & Expense Relationship Intelligence  

This epic ensures that the user understands the financial relationships with vendors and service providers.

---

### 3.2.1 Story – Supplier Performance Metrics  

The system shall track supplier-related metrics such as:

- Payment history  
- Invoice frequency  
- Average processing time  
- Outstanding liabilities  

A Supplier Reliability Score shall be computed to identify key supplier dependencies and risks.

---

### 3.2.2 Story – Expense Concentration Analysis  

The system shall analyze outgoing payments for concentration risk:

- Identify top recurring suppliers by spend  
- Detect excessive reliance on single suppliers  
- Highlight categories with high expenditure percentages  

The goal is proactive cost management and risk awareness.

---

### 3.2.3 Story – Operational Expense Insights  

The system shall provide expense dashboards showing:

- Spend by category  
- Monthly trend analysis  
- High-impact cost centers  
- Cashflow impact of scheduled payments  

The visualization must use business language, avoiding accounting complexity, enabling informed operational decisions.

---

## 3.3 Epic – Integration with Cashflow & Budgeting  

This epic ensures that expenses directly inform cashflow control and financial stability.

---

### 3.3.1 Story – Cashflow-Linked Expense Forecasting  

All recorded and scheduled expenses shall feed into the cashflow engine:

- Scheduled payment impact on liquidity  
- Recurring expense projection  
- Predictive alerts for cash shortages  

The system shall simulate how upcoming expenses affect real-time cash position.

---

### 3.3.2 Story – Expense Budgeting Module  

The system shall allow users to define operational budgets by:

- Category  
- Timeframe (weekly/monthly/quarterly)  
- Supplier or project  

Capabilities include:

- Alerting when nearing budget limits  
- Comparative analysis against historical trends  
- Automatic adjustments for recurring expenses  

This ensures disciplined spending without manual monitoring.

---

### 3.3.3 Story – Anomaly Detection & Alerts  

The platform shall detect unusual expense patterns:

- One-time spikes  
- Unexpected supplier charges  
- Deviations from recurring payment behavior  

When anomalies occur, the system shall:

- Flag the transaction  
- Notify the user  
- Assess potential cashflow impact  

---

**Feature 3 establishes outgoing financial control.**  
It ensures expenses are visible, structured, and integrated into overall cashflow management, giving the user command over every financial outflow without accounting complexity.
