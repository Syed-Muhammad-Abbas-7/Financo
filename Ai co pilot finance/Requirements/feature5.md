# Feature 5: Real-Time Business Entity & Transaction Intelligence  

This feature provides a business-centric view of all financial activity. It replaces traditional accounting structures with entity-focused intelligence that tracks customers, suppliers, products, and revenue streams in real time.

The objective is not generic reporting.  
The objective is actionable insight and control over the operational heartbeat of the business.

This feature transforms raw transactions into structured business intelligence.

---

## 5.1 Epic – Entity-Centric Transaction Mapping  

This epic organizes all financial transactions around business-relevant entities instead of accounting accounts.

---

### 5.1.1 Story – Customer & Supplier Mapping  

The system shall automatically associate each transaction with:

- Customers (revenue sources)  
- Suppliers (expense sources)  

Capabilities include:

- AI-assisted entity recognition from transaction descriptions  
- Manual override and entity creation  
- Dynamic updating as new entities are added  

**Functional Requirements:**

- Automatic tagging based on historical behavior  
- Recognition of repeat transactions  
- Cross-account entity tracking  

This ensures clarity of business relationships without accounting complexity.

---

### 5.1.2 Story – Product & Revenue Stream Classification  

The system shall categorize transactions by:

- Products sold or services delivered  
- Revenue streams or project-based income  

Capabilities include:

- Automatic inference from invoice or transaction description  
- Assignment to multiple revenue streams if applicable  
- Real-time updating of revenue contribution metrics  

This allows owners to see what drives income at an operational level.

---

### 5.1.3 Story – Expense Categorization & Tagging  

The system shall structure outgoing payments by:

- Expense category (operational, administrative, marketing, etc.)  
- Custom tags for projects, departments, or campaigns  

Capabilities include:

- AI-assisted suggestion based on historical patterns  
- Manual tag creation and overrides  
- Integration with cashflow and forecasting modules  

This provides a clear operational picture of spending.

---

## 5.2 Epic – Dynamic Relationship Intelligence  

This epic maintains evolving relationships and patterns between entities and transactions.

---

### 5.2.1 Story – Repeat Entity Recognition  

The system shall detect:

- Recurring customers and their buying patterns  
- Regular suppliers and recurring expense patterns  

The platform shall assign reliability or engagement scores to each entity to highlight strategic importance.

---

### 5.2.2 Story – Relationship-Based Alerts  

The system shall trigger notifications for:

- Unusual customer behavior (late payment, decreased purchases)  
- Supplier changes (price increases, delayed delivery)  

This allows operational intervention before cashflow or service disruptions occur.

---

### 5.2.3 Story – Multi-Entity Correlation Analysis  

The system shall analyze patterns such as:

- Revenue dependency on top customers  
- Expense concentration with key suppliers  
- Product-line profitability linked to customer segments  

Insights shall update dynamically and integrate into dashboards for instant decision clarity.

---

## 5.3 Epic – Intelligent Dashboard & Visualization  

This epic ensures that entity and transaction intelligence is actionable and digestible.

---

### 5.3.1 Story – Entity-Centric Dashboard  

The system shall present:

- Revenue by customer  
- Expenses by supplier  
- Profitability by product or service  
- High-risk entity identification  

The dashboard must:

- Avoid accounting jargon  
- Use plain business language  
- Provide drill-down capabilities  

---

### 5.3.2 Story – Trend & Performance Metrics  

The system shall calculate:

- Growth trends by customer or product  
- Supplier cost trends  
- Contribution margin and net revenue per entity  

These metrics shall be visualized through charts, heatmaps, and timelines for rapid comprehension.

---

### 5.3.3 Story – Exportable Business Insights  

The system shall allow:

- Exporting entity-level reports to PDF/Excel  
- Sharing insights with partners, accountants, or investors  
- Archiving historical entity performance comparisons  

This enables operational accountability and informed decision-making.

---

**Feature 5 establishes actionable business intelligence.**  
It transforms transactions into entity-based insights, giving the owner operational visibility and control without exposing them to complex accounting systems.
